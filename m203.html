M203 Grenade Launcher

Overview

The M203 is a single-shot, breech-loaded, pump-action grenade launcher designed to be attached to a rifle, typically the M16 or M4. It is used by the U.S. military and various other armed forces around the world for indirect fire support and is known for its versatility and effectiveness in combat scenarios.

Specifications

  • Caliber: 40x46mm
  • Length: 12.75 in (324 mm)
  • Weight: 3.5 lb (1.6 kg) (unloaded)
  • Rate of Fire: 5-7 rounds/min
  • Effective Range: 150 meters (164 yards)
  • Feed System: Single-shot, breech-loaded

How to Reload the M203

  1. Ensure the weapon is pointed in a safe direction and the safety is on.
  2. Open the breech by pulling the release lever and tilting the launcher down.
  3. Insert a 40mm grenade into the chamber.
  4. Close the breech until it clicks securely.
  5. Switch the safety to the "fire" position.
