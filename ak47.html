AK-47

Overview

The AK-47, officially known as the Avtomat Kalashnikova, is a gas-operated assault rifle that was developed in the Soviet Union by Mikhail Kalashnikov. It is one of the most widely recognized and used firearms in the world.

Specifications

  • Caliber: 7.62x39mm
  • Length: 870 mm (34.3 in)
  • Barrel Length: 415 mm (16.3 in)
  • Weight: 3.47 kg (7.7 lb) (unloaded)
  • Rate of Fire: 600 rounds/min
  • Effective Range: 300 meters (330 yards)
  • Feed System: 30-round detachable magazine

How to Reload the AK-47

  1. Ensure the weapon is pointed in a safe direction and the safety is on.
  2. Remove the empty magazine by pressing the magazine release button.
  3. Insert a fully loaded magazine into the magazine well until it clicks into place.
  4. Pull the charging handle back and release it to chamber a round.
  5. Switch the safety to the "fire" position.
