FN P90

Overview

The FN P90 is a compact personal defense weapon developed by FN Herstal. It is designed for close-quarters combat and features a unique bullpup configuration, allowing for a shorter overall length without sacrificing barrel length. The P90 is known for its high rate of fire, low recoil, and effectiveness in urban environments.

How to Reload the FN P90

  1. Ensure the weapon is pointed in a safe direction and the safety is on.
  2. Remove the empty magazine from the weapon by pressing the magazine release button.
  3. Insert a new magazine, ensuring it clicks into place.
  4. Charge the weapon by pulling the charging handle back and releasing it.
  5. Switch the safety to the "fire" position.
