M4 Carbine

Overview

The M4 Carbine is a lightweight, gas-operated, air-cooled, magazine-fed assault rifle that is a variant of the M16 rifle. It is widely used by the United States military and has become a standard issue weapon for many armed forces worldwide.

Specifications

  • Caliber: 5.56x45mm NATO
  • Length: 840 mm (33.0 in) (stock extended)
  • Barrel Length: 370 mm (14.5 in)
  • Weight: 2.88 kg (6.4 lb) (unloaded)
  • Rate of Fire: 700-950 rounds/min
  • Effective Range: 500 meters (550 yards)
  • Feed System: 30-round detachable magazine

How to Reload the M4 Carbine

  1. Ensure the weapon is pointed in a safe direction and the safety is on.
  2. Press the magazine release button to remove the empty magazine.
  3. Insert a fully loaded magazine into the magazine well until it clicks into place.
  4. Pull the charging handle back and release it to chamber a round.
  5. Switch the safety to the "fire" position.
