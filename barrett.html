Barrett M82

Overview

The Barrett M82, also known as the Barrett .50 cal, is a recoil-operated, semi-automatic sniper rifle designed by Barrett Firearms Manufacturing. It is chambered for the .50 BMG cartridge and is known for its long-range accuracy and powerful stopping power.

Specifications

  • Caliber: .50 BMG (12.7x99mm NATO)
  • Length: 1,460 mm (57.5 in)
  • Barrel Length: 660 mm (26 in)
  • Weight: 13.6 kg (30 lb) (unloaded)
  • Rate of Fire: 45 rounds/min
  • Effective Range: 1,800 meters (1,968 yards)
  • Feed System: 10-round detachable box magazine

How to Reload the Barrett M82

  1. Ensure the weapon is pointed in a safe direction and the safety is on.
  2. Press the magazine release button to remove the empty magazine.
  3. Insert a fully loaded magazine into the magazine well until it clicks into place.
  4. Pull the bolt handle back and release it to chamber a round.
  5. Switch the safety to the "fire" position.
