RPG-7 Grenade Launcher

Overview

The RPG-7 is a portable, reusable, shoulder-fired, anti-tank rocket-propelled grenade launcher developed in the Soviet Union. It is widely used by various military forces around the world due to its effectiveness against armored vehicles and fortifications, as well as its ease of use and low cost.

Specifications

  • Caliber: 40mm
  • Length: 950 mm (37.4 in)
  • Weight: 7.5 kg (16.5 lb) (unloaded)
  • Rate of Fire: 3 rounds/min
  • Effective Range: 200 meters (218 yards) against moving targets, 500 meters (547 yards) against stationary targets
  • Feed System: Single-shot, reloadable

How to Reload the RPG-7

  1. Ensure the weapon is pointed in a safe direction and the safety is on.
  2. Remove the spent rocket from the launcher by pulling it out of the tube.
  3. Insert a new rocket into the rear of the launcher, ensuring it is seated properly.
  4. Close the launcher and ensure it is secured.
  5. Switch the safety to the "fire" position.
