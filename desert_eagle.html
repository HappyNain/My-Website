Desert Eagle Pistol

Overview

The Desert Eagle is a semi-automatic pistol known for its large caliber and distinctive design. Originally developed by Magnum Research in the United States and later manufactured by Israel Military Industries, the Desert Eagle is famous for its power and is often featured in movies and video games.

Specifications

  • Caliber: .44 Magnum, .50 AE, .357 Magnum
  • Length: 10.75 in (273 mm)
  • Barrel Length: 6 in (152 mm) or 10 in (254 mm)
  • Weight: 4.5 lb (2.0 kg) (unloaded)
  • Rate of Fire: Semi-automatic
  • Effective Range: 50 meters (55 yards)
  • Feed System: 7 or 9-round detachable magazine

How to Reload the Desert Eagle

  1. Ensure the weapon is pointed in a safe direction and the safety is on.
  2. Press the magazine release button to remove the empty magazine.
  3. Insert a fully loaded magazine into the magazine well until it clicks into place.
  4. Pull the slide back and release it to chamber a round.
  5. Switch the safety to the "fire" position.
