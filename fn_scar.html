FN SCAR Rifle

Overview

The FN SCAR (Special Operations Forces Combat Assault Rifle) is a modular assault rifle designed by FN Herstal. Developed for the United States Special Operations Command, the SCAR is known for its versatility, reliability, and adaptability to various combat scenarios.

Specifications

  • Caliber: 5.56x45mm NATO or 7.62x51mm NATO
  • Length: 838 mm (33 in) (SCAR-L) or 1,000 mm (39.4 in) (SCAR-H)
  • Barrel Length: 350 mm (13.8 in) (SCAR-L) or 400 mm (15.7 in) (SCAR-H)
  • Weight: 3.6 kg (8.0 lb) (unloaded)
  • Rate of Fire: 600 rounds/min
  • Effective Range: 500 meters (547 yards)
  • Feed System: 20 or 30-round detachable magazines

How to Reload the FN SCAR

  1. Ensure the weapon is pointed in a safe direction and the safety is on.
  2. Press the magazine release button to remove the empty magazine.
  3. Insert a fully loaded magazine into the magazine well until it clicks into place.
  4. Pull the charging handle back and release it to chamber a round.
  5. Switch the safety to the "fire" position.
